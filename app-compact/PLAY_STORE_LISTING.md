## Full Description (4000 chars)
📸 **Scan → Split → Settle — the smartest travel expense app.**

Travel Expense is an AI-first shared expense tracker built for travelers. Snap a receipt, let AI extract every item, assign items to people, and settle up — all in seconds.

**Why switch from Splitwise?**
• **AI receipt itemization** — even paid Splitwise can't do this. Scan a restaurant receipt and AI extracts each line item. Assign items to people with one tap.
• **Unlimited everything, free** — no daily add cap, no paywall on currency conversion, charts, search, or export.
• **Real travel budget pacing** — see "you're ¥X under pace today" on a live dashboard. Splitwise has no budgeting.
• **Per-expense FX accuracy** — each expense converts at the rate on its date, not today's stale rate.
• **Multi-modal capture** — photo / voice / email / screenshot, 18 languages, Cantonese-aware.
• **Beautiful washi-paper + liquid-glass design** — modern, fast, mobile-first.

**Core Features:**
• 📷 AI receipt OCR (photo, voice, email, screenshot)
• 👥 Per-item assignment — tap faces to split by item
• 💰 Equal / weighted / exact / percentage / adjustment splits
• 🧾 Multiple payers per expense
• 📊 Live budget pacing dashboard
• 🔄 Supabase cloud sync + Notion mirror
• 🌐 17 currencies with live FX rates
• 📤 CSV/JSON export, search, charts
• 🔒 Private/代付 expenses
• 🤝 Shared trip ledger with roles (owner/admin/editor/viewer)
• 💬 Expense comments + activity feed
• 🔁 Recurring expenses

**No account required to start.** Sign in with Google or email to sync across devices.
